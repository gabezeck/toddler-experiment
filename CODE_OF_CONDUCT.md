# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

* **Respect** differing viewpoints, experiences, and expressions
* **Gracefully accept** constructive criticism
* **Focus** on what is best for the community
* **Show empathy** toward other community members
* **Be collaborative** and work together
* **Ask for help** when you need it
* **Help others** when you can

### Unacceptable Behavior

* **Harassment** in any form, including:
  - Offensive or derogatory comments
  - Personal attacks or insults
  - Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
  - Deliberate intimidation or stalking
  - Sharing private information without consent
* **Discriminatory** language or actions
* **Disruptive** behavior that interferes with others' work
* **Publishing** private information without permission
* **Any other conduct** that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, including:
- GitHub repositories
- Issue threads and pull requests
- Discussion forums and chats
- Any other community-organized events

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces. Examples of representing our community include using an official e-mail address, posting via an official social media account, or acting as an appointed representative at an online or offline event.

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you witness or experience any violations of this Code of Conduct, please report it to the project maintainers.

**How to Report:**
1. **Email:** [your-email@example.com](mailto:your-email@example.com)
2. **GitHub:** Send a private message to any maintainer

**What to Include:**
- Your contact information (so we can follow up)
- The nature of the violation
- Description of the incident, including:
  - Individuals involved
  - When and where it occurred
  - Any witnesses or evidence
- Any other relevant information

### Confidentiality

All reports will be kept confidential to the extent possible. We will respect your privacy and only share information with those who need to know to address the situation.

### Response

The project team is committed to promptly reviewing and investigating all complaints.

**Potential Actions:**
- **Private warning** to clarify the nature of the violation
- **Request for a public apology**
- **Temporary suspension** from the community
- **Permanent ban** from the community

The action taken will depend on the severity of the violation and whether there's a pattern of behavior.

We will:
1. Acknowledge receipt of the report within 48 hours
2. Investigate the situation
3. Determine appropriate action
4. Follow up with the reporter

We will NOT:
- Discuss the report publicly
- Share the reporter's identity without consent
- Retaliate against anyone who makes a good-faith report
